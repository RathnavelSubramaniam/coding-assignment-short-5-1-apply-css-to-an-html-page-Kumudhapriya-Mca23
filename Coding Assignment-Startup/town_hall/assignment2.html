New York Times columnist and author,Andrew Ross Sorkin,has been described as "the most famous financial journalist of his generation" A leading voice on Wall Street and corporate America,his New York Times bestseller,Too Big to Fall,was the first true,behind-the-scenes,moment-by-moment account of how that that financial crisis developed into a global tsunami.

The Economics,The Financial Times,and Business Week all named Too Big To Fail 2009.The book was adapted as a movie by HBO Films and premiered on HBO on May 23,2011.The film was directed by Curtis Handson,and the screenplay was written by peter Gould
